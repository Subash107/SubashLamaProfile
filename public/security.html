📜 Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

I am a cybersecurity analyst and take the security of this site seriously. If you discover a vulnerability, I encourage responsible disclosure and will respond promptly.

What I ask of researchers

  • Report issues by email before public disclosure — give me reasonable time to fix it
  • Do not access, modify, or exfiltrate data beyond what is needed to demonstrate the vulnerability
  • Do not run automated scanners against the live site in a way that degrades availability
  • Include enough detail to reproduce the issue (steps, payload, screenshots if relevant)

What I commit to

  • Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
  • Provide an initial assessment within 5 business days
  • Keep you updated on remediation progress
  • Credit you in the acknowledgements section of this page (if you wish)
  • No legal action for good-faith disclosures that follow this policy
